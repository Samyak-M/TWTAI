# How to Add a Team Member

## Overview

This guide explains how to add a new team member to your workspace. You can simply leverage the admin panel to execute this task.

## Prerequisites

- You must be a workspace admin
- The new member's email address

## Steps

1. Sign in to the admin panel at `https://app.example.com/admin`.
2. Click **Team** in the left sidebar.
3. Click **Invite member**.
4. Enter the new member's email address and select their role.
5. Click **Send invite**.

The new member will receive an email with a login link. They can whitelist the sender address if the email is filtered.

## Result

The new member appears in your team list with a "Pending" status until they accept the invite. Their status will be updated by the system once the invite has been accepted.
